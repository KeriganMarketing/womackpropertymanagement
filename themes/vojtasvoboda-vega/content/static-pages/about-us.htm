At Womack, our goal is to make your ownership as seamless, stress free and profitable as possible. We provide asset and facilities management services for:

  • Industrial Properties
  • Office Properties
  • Retail & Shopping Centers
  • Residential Properties

 Our services can be customized to meet your financial and physical needs, including:

  • Performing detailed property inspections on a routine basis and providing a monthly written status summary
  • Contracting for services, repairs and maintenance on the property as authorized
  • Managing service contracts, monitoring insurance certificates, and maintaining scope of work required by contracts
  • Soliciting and bidding service contracts to maintain quality and market pricing
  • Accounting for all income and expenses produced by the property
  • Responding in a timely manner to the needs of your tenants
  • Reconciling operating expenses and billing tenants as per lease requirements
  • Providing bookkeeping and financial reporting for the property
  • Preparing annual budgets
  • Marketing, leasing and negotiating leases for the property

As an experienced Property Manager with a demonstrated 30-year history of working in the commercial and residential real estate industry, I am able to provide a wide range of expertise in managing your asset to the utmost potential with skills including:

  • Customer Service Excellence
  • Conflict Resolution
  • Contract Negotiation
  • Lease Administration
  • Financial Reporting
  • Accounting Administration
  • CAM Reconciliation
  • Accounts Receivable/Collections
  • Accounts Payable
  • Budgets & Business Plan (develop & implement)
  • Vendor Procurement—Bid Process to Contract
  • Project Management—Capital/Tenant Improvements
  • Real Estate Development
  • Real Estate Due Diligence—Disposition
